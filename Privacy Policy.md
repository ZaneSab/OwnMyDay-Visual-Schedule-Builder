OwnMyDay Schedule Builder (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using the app.

Information We Collect
Account Information

Email address and authentication details when you create an account or sign in (via Firebase Authentication).
User Content

Schedules you create.
Custom tiles you create, including photos you take and upload for personal tile use.
Device and Usage Information

Basic device identifiers used to enforce plan device limits.
App activity related to subscription status and entitlement checks.
Subscription Information

Subscription and entitlement status (for example, Pro access) processed through RevenueCat and app store billing providers (Google Play / Apple App Store).
How We Use Information
We use your information to:

Create and manage your account.
Save and sync your schedules.
Let Pro users create, upload, and use personal photo tiles.
Enforce plan limits (for example, schedule and device caps).
Process and verify subscriptions and restore purchases.
Maintain app security, reliability, and functionality.
Camera and Photo Access
If you grant permission, the app uses:

Camera access to capture custom tile photos.
Photo library access (where applicable) to crop/edit selected images.
These images are used only for your custom tiles and are stored in your account-specific storage area.

Where Data Is Stored
Your data may be stored and processed using:

Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage)
RevenueCat (subscription management)
Google Play / Apple App Store (billing and subscription processing)
Sharing of Information
We do not sell personal information.

We share information only with service providers needed to operate the app, including:

Firebase (Google)
RevenueCat
Platform billing providers (Google Play / Apple)
These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

Data Retention
We keep your data while your account is active or as needed to provide the app.
You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Security
We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data appropriately.

Children’s Privacy
The app may be used by families, educators, and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate authorization by a parent/guardian where required by law. If you believe a child has provided personal data improperly, contact us.

Your Rights and Choices
Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

Access your personal data
